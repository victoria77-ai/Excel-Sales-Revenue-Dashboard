# Corporate Sales & Revenue Performance Dashboard

Interactive Excel dashboard and beginner data analytics portfolio project focused on regional sales performance, product profitability, and monthly revenue trends.

## 📝 Project Overview
This data analytics project transforms raw transactional sales data into an interactive executive dashboard. The goal was to generate key business insights into regional sales performance, product profitability, salesperson performance, and monthly revenue trends to support data-driven decision-making.

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset contains transactional sales records with the following key fields:
- Order ID
- Order Date
- Region
- Salesperson
- Product
- Category
- Units Sold
- Unit Price
- Revenue
- Cost
- Profit [2]

## ✅ Tasks Performed
- Converted raw data into an Excel Table
- Applied formatting and conditional formatting
- Calculated Total Revenue, Total Cost, Total Profit, and Profit Margin
- Found Average Revenue and Average Profit
- Counted the number of orders
- Used SUMIF and COUNTIF by Region
- Used XLOOKUP to retrieve product details
- Created Pivot Tables for Revenue by Region and Profit by Product
- Performed monthly sales analysis
- Created column, line, and pie charts
- Identified the best-selling product
- Identified the salesperson with the highest profit
- Built an interactive dashboard with slicers
- Highlighted high-profit orders [2]

## 📌 Key KPIs
- Total Revenue: £750,830
- Total Profit: £210,700
- Total Cost: £540,130
- Total Units Sold: 2,172
- Profit Margin: 28%

## 🔑 Key Insights
- Total revenue reached £750,830, generating £210,700 in total profit.
- The West region contributed the highest overall revenue.
- Laptop was the most profitable product.
- Headphones were the best-selling product by units sold.
- July recorded the strongest monthly performance.
- Alice was the top salesperson by total profit generated.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
- Prioritize targeted sales and marketing efforts within the West region.
- Increase focus on Laptop sales due to their strong profitability.
- Use Headphones as a volume-driving product in marketing promotions.
- Analyze the top salesperson’s strategy and share best practices across the team.
- Review weaker months and underperforming regions to improve consistency.
